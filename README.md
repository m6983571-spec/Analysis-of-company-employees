# Analysis-of-company-employees
HR Analytics Dashboard: Insights & Attrition Analysis
I developed a comprehensive HR dashboard for ATLAS Labs to monitor the employee lifecycle, from hiring trends to attrition risks.

Key Highlights:
Workforce Overview: Tracked 1,400+ total employees with an overall attrition rate of 0.84.

Attrition Drivers: Identified that Frequent Travel and Overtime are the primary reasons for staff turnover.

Retention Risk: Discovered that the highest turnover occurs within the first year (0.4 rate) of employment.

Departmental Insights: Analyzed performance and satisfaction across Technology, Sales, and HR, noting that HR roles currently face the highest turnover.

Demographic Mapping: Profiled the workforce by age (majority 20-29), marital status, and ethnicity to ensure diversity and salary equity.

Performance Tracking: Built a system to monitor Manager vs. Self-ratings and satisfaction levels over time to predict "at-risk" employees before they leave.
